About Us

Good Cause is a Bangladeshi charity that provides aid regardless of race, religion or gender identity to those who are victims of natural disasters or conflict or suffering from poverty, hunger, disease, illiteracy, discrimination, homelessness, debt, unemployment, injustice, deprivation or lack of skills and economic opportunities.
